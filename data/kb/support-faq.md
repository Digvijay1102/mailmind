# Frequently Asked Questions - Customer Support

## Account & Billing

### How do I reset my password?

Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. You'll receive a link to set a new password within 5 minutes.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. For enterprise accounts, we also support invoicing.

### How can I update my billing information?

Log in to your account, navigate to Settings > Billing, and update your payment method or billing address. Changes take effect immediately for new charges.

### What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual subscriptions. For monthly subscriptions, please contact support@company.com with your request.

## Technical Support

### The system seems slow. What should I do?

First, try refreshing your browser and clearing your cache. If the issue persists, check your internet connection speed. Users on slow connections may experience delays. For persistent issues, contact support with your account email.

### I'm getting an error code ERR_401. What does this mean?

This error indicates unauthorized access, usually due to an expired session. Try logging out completely and logging back in. If the problem continues, reset your password.

### How long does data synchronization take?

Initial data sync typically takes 2-5 minutes depending on the size of your dataset. Subsequent updates sync in real-time to within 30 seconds.

### Do you have API documentation?

Yes! Our complete API documentation is available at docs.company.com/api. It includes authentication guide, endpoint reference, and code examples in multiple languages.

## Account Management

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Visit Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan. Your access will remain until the end of your billing period. You can export your data at any time during your subscription.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Changes to your plan take effect immediately. You'll receive a prorated credit or charge on your next billing date.

### Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. Contact sales@company.com for volume discounts.

## Data & Security

### Is my data encrypted?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+) and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We comply with SOC 2 Type II standards.

### How do I export my data?

You can export data in CSV or JSON format from Settings > Data Export. Exports are generated within 24 hours and remain available for 30 days.

### What is your data retention policy?

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion.
